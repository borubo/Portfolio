My resolve -July 28, 2020-

　In 2012 I graduated from technical college and have been working in manufacturing for 8 years.
　However, the performance of the manufacturing industry in recent years has been poor and the future is uncertain. This year, I decided to become an IT engineer.
　Japanese manufacturing industry requires a lot of routine work and paperwork, and requires more interaction than necessary. For this reason, I think it's difficult to increase your market value as an engineer because you have to spend a lot of time doing things other than technology.
　In order to solve these problems, IT skills are essential. That's why I want to develop rational systems and services.
In the future, I would like to continue learning and practicing to become a person with high market value.
